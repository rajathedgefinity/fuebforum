Why opt for Fueb home automation system?

We have an enviable line up of products which are world class in function yet affordable to all the home owners. Our motto is to connect the world in a sustainable manner thus making living safer and convenient. Fueb believes in automating what can be automated, which makes your home smart and energy efficient. It reduces the time you spend on manual work, giving you more opportunities to strengthen bonds and expand your horizons.
Members of a home now spend more time in different locations with a need for a technology-enabled system that will ensure the safety of their homes while they are away. Your home is certainly your most prized possession. At Fueb, we have designed automation solutions for end-to-end security, lifestyle automation and enhanced convenience. Our motto is simple, your home is entirely taken care off when you are not there.

How does Fueb work?

Products by Fueb let you be in charge of your house even from a remote location. You will be able to access your home appliances from anywhere via Fueb app. Fueb, on your personal device, can lock and unlock the door for you, switch on or off the lights and also our VDB Smart Door Bell keeps a check who is at the door without you being there and sends you an alert when something unusual takes place.

Will my house look clumsy once I install Fueb?

As much as it sounds awesome, Fueb has made sure that you are spared of the bulkiness and ungainly sights of appliances all over your haven instead give a good artistic look and feel. Extremely sleek and stylish world class designs will blend in with your interiors effortlessly. To add to your aesthetics, you can even customize the colour scheme.

How does a home automation system help?

Gone are those days when our physical presence was needed to manage house appliances like switch off/on lights, lock/unlock doors, and check if everything is in order before leaving for a business trip or a Holiday Vacation. A smart home, powered by intelligent technology will enable you to be at peace without compromising on your home’s security with you being in a completely different location. The systems are designed to also be helpful and come in very handy when you would want to remotely help you kids at home, elderly people and your loved ones who may need your assistance at any time of the day.

How do Fueb Touch-Magic switches work?

Enabled by voice activated technology Fueb's Touch-Magic switches let you manage your home as you speak. Manually with feather touch or via Fueb app. Its versatile designs can be chosen and customized to suit your taste; the Touch-Magic switches are also compatible with Google Homes and Amazon Alexa.

I am worried that my system’s security is at stake if I opt for a home automation system.

Our automation system can be used and accessed via a secured app that ensures complete operational safety. Rest assured, your security will not be compromised or tampered with. We have embedded and followed highly secure and trusted safety protocols while designing and developing our products & applications whether it is to do with the data communication and or with the device safety protocols to not to be hacked.

How do Armour smart lockswork?

Fueb’s Armour smart locks are enabled with advanced biometric and RFID based access control. Besides assuring top-notch security, these locks are extremely convenient to use, helping you manage entry/exit and much more all from your smartphone.

How do Fueb’s Smart doorbell work?

Vibe is not just your ordinary doorbell. With an in-built video and audio feature, you can instantly view and communicate who has arrived at your main door or gate. To add to your security, it can also alert you of suspicious activities. Vibe doorbells can be easily managed from your smartphone via the Fueb app. And it is water resistant; you can install outdoor to withstand outdoor weather conditions.

Is it compulsory to buy all Fueb devices to set up a smart home?

You can choose to install one or all Fueb solution based on your automation requirement. However, multiple Fueb devices can work together to help you set up a great home automation system and make the most of it.

How can I operate Fueb devices from a wireless range?

Fueb devices work seamlessly from a wireless range of with a good range to access them subject to construction of the building. Also, when operating on a wireless mode, our products do not interfere with other devices in the same zone.

How soon should I replace the batteries of Armour door locks?

For a General usage, the Armour door locks come in with a battery life of up to 8-10 months, As a smart feature, you will get a warning on your phone when the battery reaches 25% of its capacity. Replacing the batteries is easy and takes a few minutes of your time and saves you of further hassles and defunct door locks.
